METRO CITY FITNESS - COMPREHENSIVE MEMBER HANDBOOK & OPERATIONS GUIDE
Last Revised: November 2025
Document ID: MCF-POL-2025-V4

SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE
1.1 Welcome
Welcome to Metro City Fitness (MCF). This handbook serves as a definitive guide to the rights, responsibilities, and regulations governing your membership. By accessing the facility, you agree to abide by these policies.

1.2 Mission Statement
To provide a safe, inclusive, and high-performance environment for all members. We value integrity, hygiene, and mutual respect.

SECTION 2: OPERATING HOURS AND ACCESS
2.1 Standard Hours
- Monday - Friday: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Saturday - Sunday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Public Holidays: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Unless otherwise posted).
- Closed: Christmas Day (Dec 25) and New Year's Day (Jan 1).

2.2 Check-In Procedures
- All members must scan their RFID Key Fob or Digital App Barcode at the front turnstile upon entry.
- "Tailgating" (allowing another person to enter behind you without scanning) is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate $50 fine and possible membership suspension.
- Lost Key Fobs can be replaced for a fee of $15.

2.3 Peak Hours
- Peak hours are defined as Monday-Friday, 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM.
- During peak hours, members are required to limit cardio machine usage to 30 minutes if others are waiting.

SECTION 3: MEMBERSHIP TIERS AND BENEFITS
3.1 Basic Membership
- Access: Single location only.
- Amenities: General gym floor, locker rooms, showers.
- Guest Privileges: None (Guests pay $15 per visit).
- Classes: Excluded (Drop-in fee applies).

3.2 Premium Membership
- Access: All Metro City Fitness locations nationwide.
- Amenities: Includes Sauna, Steam Room, and Tanning.
- Guest Privileges: One (1) free guest per visit. Guest must be accompanied by the member.
- Classes: Unlimited access to Group X classes (Yoga, HIIT, Spin).

3.3 Corporate & Student Plans
- Valid ID required annually for verification.
- Student discount (15%) applies only to Basic Membership.
- Corporate plans require a minimum of 5 active employees from the same registered company.

SECTION 4: PAYMENT AND BILLING
4.1 Dues
- Membership dues are billed bi-weekly or monthly via EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer).
- A late fee of $25 applies if payment fails and is not rectified within 7 days.

4.2 Freezing Membership
- Members may freeze their account for medical reasons (with doctor's note) for up to 3 months at $0 cost.
- Voluntary freezes (vacation, etc.) cost $10/month and are limited to 3 months per calendar year.
- Freeze requests must be submitted 5 days before the billing cycle.

4.3 Cancellation Policy
- Standard Cancellation: Requires 30 days' written notice via email to support@metrocityfitness.com or in-person form.
- Early Termination Fee: If cancelled within the first 6 months of a 12-month contract, a $50 fee applies.
- Move-Away Clause: Fee waived if member provides proof of relocation >25 miles from any MCF facility.

SECTION 5: GUEST POLICY
5.1 Guest Registration
- All guests must present valid Photo ID (Driver's License or Passport).
- Guests must sign a Liability Waiver before entering the workout floor.
- Guests under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian at all times.

5.2 Conduct
- Members are responsible for the conduct of their guests.
- If a guest violates gym rules (e.g., dropping weights, aggression), the hosting member's membership may be jeopardized.

SECTION 6: CODE OF CONDUCT & ETIQUETTE
6.1 Respect
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind towards staff or members is zero-tolerance. Immediate expulsion will occur.
- No soliciting or selling of personal training services by non-employed trainers.

6.2 Equipment Usage
- "Working in" is encouraged during rest periods.
- Do not monopolize equipment while texting or browsing social media.
- Re-rack all weights, dumbbells, and plates after use. Failure to re-rack weights >50lbs may result in a verbal warning.

6.3 Noise
- Grunting is permitted but should be kept to a reasonable volume.
- Slamming weights is prohibited unless on a designated lifting platform with bumper plates.
- Headphones are mandatory for listening to music. Bluetooth speakers are banned.

SECTION 7: DRESS CODE AND HYGIENE
7.1 Attire
- Athletic shoes (closed-toe) must be worn at all times. No boots, sandals, crocs, or socks-only (except in Yoga studio).
- Shirts must be worn. "Shirtless" training is prohibited to prevent sweat transmission to benches.
- Jeans, cargo pants, or belts with metal buckles are not permitted as they damage upholstery.

7.2 Hygiene
- Please use deodorant.
- Wipe down equipment BEFORE and AFTER use with provided disinfectant wipes/spray.
- Do not apply heavy perfumes or colognes (many members have sensitivities).

SECTION 8: LOCKER ROOMS AND SAUNA
8.1 Lockers
- Lockers are for day-use only. Items left overnight are removed.
- MCF is not responsible for lost or stolen items. Use a high-quality padlock.

8.2 Showers & Sauna
- Towels: Not provided (unless rented for $2). Bring your own.
- Shaving is strictly prohibited in showers, steam rooms, and saunas.
- Sauna Etiquette: No shoes, no drying clothes on the heater. Limit session to 15 minutes for safety.

SECTION 9: SAFETY AND EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS
9.1 Medical Emergencies
- AEDs (Automated External Defibrillators) are located at the Front Desk and Cardio Zone.
- In case of injury, notify staff immediately. Do not attempt to move a person with potential spinal injury.
- First Aid kits are available at the reception.

9.2 Fire Evacuation
- In the event of an alarm, cease exercise immediately.
- Exit via the nearest marked Fire Exit (North and South doors).
- Gather at the designated assembly point in the main parking lot.

9.3 Spotting
- Staff are not permitted to spot heavy lifts due to liability. Please ask a fellow member or use safety bars in power racks.

SECTION 10: PERSONAL TRAINING
10.1 Certified Trainers
- Only MCF-employed Personal Trainers are insured to train clients.
- Outside trainers bringing clients to train will be asked to leave and memberships may be revoked.

10.2 Packages
- Training sessions expire 6 months from purchase date.
- 24-hour notice is required for cancellation of a session to avoid being charged.

SECTION 11: CHILDCARE (KIDS CLUB)
11.1 Hours and Age
- Available Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM.
- Ages: 6 months to 12 years.
- Maximum stay: 2 hours per day.

11.2 Health
- Sick children (fever, runny nose, cough) will not be admitted.
- Parents must remain on the gym premises while child is in Kids Club.

SECTION 12: LOST AND FOUND
- Valuables (wallets, phones, keys) are held in a safe for 30 days.
- Clothing and water bottles are held for 7 days, then donated to charity.
- MCF is not responsible for items left in the Lost and Found bin.

SECTION 13: FACILITY MAINTENANCE
13.1 Closures
- MCF reserves the right to close areas of the gym for maintenance/repairs.
- Notification will be sent via email/app for planned outages >24 hours.

13.2 Reporting
- Please report broken equipment to the front desk or via the member app using the QR codes on machines.

SECTION 14: LEGAL DISCLAIMER
14.1 Liability Waiver
By signing your membership agreement, you acknowledge that exercise involves physical risk. You assume all responsibility for injury, illness, or death that may result from using the facility.

14.2 Privacy Policy
We value your privacy. Your biometric data (check-in logs) and payment info are encrypted. We do not sell member data to third parties. Security cameras are in operation 24/7 for safety purposes.
